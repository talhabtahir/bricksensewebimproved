About Our App

Smart Brick & Wall Quality Inspection

BrickSense is an AI-driven quality inspection platform for the construction
industry, designed to simplify and modernize how brick quality is assessed.
Traditional methods of testing bricks are time-consuming, costly, and require
specialized equipment. BrickSense solves this by using computer vision and
machine learning to analyze a simple photo of a brick or wall.

  • Saves time and cost
  • Ensures quality & durability
  • Instant decision making
  • Suitable for single bricks or entire walls
  • Works from any device with internet connection.

Quick FAQs

Got questions? We've got bricks of wisdom

What does the app actually do?
Our app uses advanced image analysis to inspect bricks and walls for cracks, misalignments, and surface defects. Just snap a photo or upload one, and get instant feedback on structural integrity.
Do I need any special equipment to use the app?
Nope! All you need is a smartphone or tablet with a camera. The app is designed to work with standard mobile devices—no extra tools required.
Can it inspect entire walls or just individual bricks?
Both! You can analyze single bricks for close-up inspection or capture larger sections of a wall to assess overall quality and detect widespread issues.
Does the app work offline?
No, the app requires an internet connection to function. All image analysis is performed in the cloud to ensure high accuracy and fast results. However, no images or personal data are stored—your photos are processed securely and then discarded immediately after inspection.
Is this app suitable for professional use?
Absolutely. Whether you're a contractor, site manager, or quality inspector, the app provides reliable data and exportable reports to support professional-grade assessments.
